Video Editing

Learn how to cut, enhance, and transform raw footage into professional, cinematic videos using industry-standard tools and techniques.

Learning Roadmap

Beginner Level

  • Understanding video formats & resolutions
  • Basic cuts, trims, and timeline editing
  • Importing audio, images, and clips
  • Simple transitions & text titles

Intermediate Level

  • Color correction & color grading
  • Audio balancing & noise reduction
  • Speed ramps & motion effects
  • Creative storytelling techniques

Advanced Level

  • Cinematic transitions
  • Visual effects & compositing
  • Green screen (chroma key)
  • Exporting for YouTube, Instagram & Reels

Tools & Software

Adobe Premiere Pro After Effects DaVinci Resolve Final Cut Pro CapCut

Skills You Will Gain

  • Professional video storytelling
  • Color grading for cinematic look
  • Sound design & audio sync
  • Editing for social media platforms
  • Client-ready video production

Practice & Next Steps

  • Edit a short cinematic reel (30–60 sec)
  • Create YouTube intro & outro
  • Replicate a trending Instagram reel
  • Build a personal video portfolio
